Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data safe in SpendNote?
Yes. All your notes and expenses are stored locally on your device. SpendNote does not collect or share personal data.
How can I secure my notes?
You can enable App Lock or Fingerprint authentication from Settings to protect sensitive notes and information.
What happens if I forget my app lock PIN?
For security reasons, locked data cannot be recovered if the PIN or biometric access is lost.
Are my notes and expenses backed up?
Currently, SpendNote stores data only on your device. Backup and restore features may be added in future updates.
How does expense tracking work?
You can add daily expenses from the Money section. SpendNote helps you keep track of spending in a simple and organized way.
How does bill splitting work?
Add participants and expenses, and SpendNote automatically calculates each person’s share.
Can I recover deleted notes or expenses?
Deleted notes or expenses cannot be restored. Please review carefully before deleting.
